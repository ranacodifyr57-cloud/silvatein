Botanical · Editorial

Who we are

A label for the modern eastern wardrobe

Silvatein is a premium women's apparel label built around the joy of getting dressed. We pair traditional silhouettes — kurtas, frocks and three-piece suits — with a quiet, editorial sensibility.

What started as a passion shared on Instagram has grown into a community of women across Pakistan and beyond who return season after season. Every order is personal; many are made just for you.

What we stand for

Considered, not mass-produced

Considered craft

Small batches and made-to-order pieces, finished by hand with attention to drape and detail.

Earthy palette

Prints and tones drawn from nature — sage, clay, marigold and rust — that feel timeless rather than seasonal.

Worldwide reach

Rooted in Pakistan, shipped across the globe so our pieces find you wherever you are.
